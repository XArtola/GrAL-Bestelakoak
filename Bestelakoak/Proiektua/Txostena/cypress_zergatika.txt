\section*{Description}
The pommelo (\textit{Citrus maxima} or \textit{Citrus grandis}) is the largest citrus fruit from the family Rutaceae and the principal ancestor of the grapefruit.

\section*{Characteristics}
\begin{itemize}
    \item Largest citrus fruit
    \item Sweet taste
    \item Thick rind
\end{itemize}
